What really matters when making mobile application development technology decisions?

  • Explore MADPs but do not consider them a replacement for a piecemeal strategy.
  • Accept that you will use native and Web technologies to build great apps. This is not a choice.
  • Cultivate a high degree of expertise with Web technologies, since this is the common denominator.
  • Invest in UX. No platform will prevent poor design decisions.
